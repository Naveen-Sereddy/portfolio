FinFlow · Design board

The whole company's spend, in flow.

48 screens · 3 roles · 1 shared workspace

FinFlow is the expense-management workspace built for Reyonal — a growing SaaS company (100–500 employees) that had outgrown receipt-by-Slack but wasn't ready for an enterprise ERP. Teams in this band lose 6–10 finance hours a month to receipt chasing, plus another 4–6 hours per monthly close — and often don't catch policy violations until quarterly review. Personas and workflows here were derived from stakeholder interviews across Reyonal's finance, sales, and marketing teams.

Product vision: "The whole company's spend, in flow." One workspace where a Finance Admin can see, decide, and close the month without leaving for QuickBooks, Slack, or Excel. Employees submit in 30 seconds on mobile. Managers approve in 2 clicks. Auditors get an immutable trail.

  • 30+ desktop screens across submission, approval, cards, reimbursements, reports, settings, and audit
  • An 8-screen mobile flow for the on-the-go employee
  • Light + dark themes defined as tokens from day one
  • WCAG 2.1 AA across every surface
