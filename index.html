02 · About

I’m a designer who treats research as the first draft of the interface.

With 2+ years across UX and front-end, I work the full arc: talking to users, mapping information architecture, wireframing, prototyping, and testing until the flow feels obvious. Then I help build it.

I care about the unglamorous parts: accessible contrast, sensible defaults, fast load times, and copy that doesn’t make people think. Good design should disappear into the task.
